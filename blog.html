The Complete Guide to Aftercare for Your New Tattoo

Proper aftercare is crucial for maintaining the beauty and health of your new tattoo. The first 24 hours require keeping the protective bandage on, followed by establishing a careful cleaning routine with mild soap and lukewarm water. Regular moisturizing with appropriate products is essential during healing, which typically takes 15-30 days. Avoid swimming, direct sunlight, and scratching during this period. The healing process includes initial redness and swelling, followed by itching and peeling. Long-term care involves sun protection and moisturizing. Watch for signs of infection and seek help if complications arise. Following these guidelines ensures your tattoo remains vibrant for years.
