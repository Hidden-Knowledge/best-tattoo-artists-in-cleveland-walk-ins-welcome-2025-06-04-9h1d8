About CLE Tatted

Founded in 2015 by master tattoo artist Mike Stevens, CLE Tatted began as a small studio dedicated to bringing world-class tattoo artistry to Cleveland. What started as a two-artist shop has grown into Cleveland's premier tattoo destination, known for our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Our mission is to provide exceptional tattoo artistry in a welcoming, professional environment where creativity flourishes and clients' visions come to life. We value artistic integrity, safety, and customer satisfaction above all else, ensuring every client leaves our studio proud of their new piece of art.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a tattoo cost?

Pricing varies based on size, complexity, and placement. Small pieces start at $100, while larger custom work is priced by the hour. We provide detailed quotes during consultations to ensure transparency.

What should I expect during my first tattoo session?

Your artist will review the design, placement, and sizing with you. They'll prepare the area, transfer the design, and guide you through the process. Sessions can last anywhere from 30 minutes to several hours, depending on the piece.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes! We welcome walk-ins during our business hours (10 AM - 8 PM, Tuesday through Saturday). While availability varies, we'll do our best to accommodate you or schedule a future appointment.

What about aftercare?

We provide detailed written and verbal aftercare instructions. This includes cleaning procedures, moisturizing recommendations, and activities to avoid. We're available for follow-up questions and offer free touch-ups within the first month if needed.
